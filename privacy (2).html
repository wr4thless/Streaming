Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This app ("the App") is a personal automation tool built and operated by a single individual for their own use.

Data the App Accesses

The App uses an authorization token, granted directly by the operator through TikTok's own login screen, solely to post video content to the operator's own TikTok account. The App does not access, read, or store data belonging to any other TikTok user.

Data Storage

Authorization tokens are stored locally on the operator's own device and are never transmitted to or shared with any third party.

Data Sharing

No data collected or used by the App is sold, rented, or shared with any third party for advertising or any other purpose.

Contact
